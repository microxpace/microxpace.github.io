OUR STORY

Our team comes with the experience and knowledge

MicroXpace is a group of scientists who believe that microbes are the most compelling force on earth with the power to solve major problems faced by our planet.

Extend the use of microbe-based practices and the most-modern technologies instead to solve latent problems of social and environmental development associated with animal health.

We provide specialized services in the identification and characterization of problems associated with disease-causing agents in animals and resident microbial communities, and we offer alternative products and solutions based on beneficial microbes.

Indeed, we aim to introduce our products as a standardized and everyday practice in the animal health sector.
