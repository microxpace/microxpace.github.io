Connecting ideas

We are a non-profit organization that aim to inquire in the vast world of microbiota by creating an environment where new and old ideas can merge. Sharing and complementing ideas will bring us further in the knowledge of microbial interactions.

Adapting pipelines

We have in our hands pipelines to analyse and decipher complicated microbial interactions. But just like microbial species co-evolves with their hosts, we are also prepared to ‘co-evolve’ our pipelines for the better understanding of each host-microbiota relationship.

Building communities

We want to bring closer researchers that are passionate about microbiota and create a community, as microbial species do, where joint work can promote our understanding about microbiota.

Translating science

Big solutions from tiny partners!

We want to bring closer researchers that are passionate about microbiota and create a community, as microbial species do, where joint work can promote our understanding about microbiota. We believe that beneficial microbial species within host microbiota hold the key for the solution of infectious diseases. Identifying these microbial species is the first step to move towards technologies that will improve human and animal health.
